### ONE-LINE EXPERIMENT NAME
MTG Commander Deck Upgrades Sales Test

### PROBLEM/OPPORTUNITY
Identify if there is a demand for selling upgraded versions of MTG Commander decks to existing HappyHomarid customers.

### TARGET SILO
HappyHomarid Storefront - Existing Buyers List (156 previous buyers)

### HYPOTHESIS
Existing HappyHomarid customers who have purchased MTG Commander decks in the past are likely interested in purchasing upgraded versions of those decks directly through DMs.

### 60-MINUTE STEPS

1. **Preparation**:
   - Review existing customer data to segment potential buyers.
   - Design a compelling product description for the deck upgrades, including before-and-after images and pricing details.

2. **DM Composition**:
   - Compose personalized direct messages (DMs) for 50 randomly selected customers from the previous buyer list.
     - Include product highlights.
     - Mention special offers or discounts to increase interest.
   - Ensure DMs are engaging, addressing each customer by name and highlighting their past purchase history.

3. **Launch**:
   - Send out the personalized DMs at a strategic time (e.g., during a regular business day).
   - Monitor responses in real-time for immediate engagement or feedback.

4. **Follow-Up**:
   - For customers who respond positively, follow up with additional information and offer them a limited-time discount.
   - For those who are interested but need more convincing, provide detailed product information and testimonials.

5. **Close the Loop**:
   - After 24 hours of sending DMs, close the loop by summarizing responses and actions taken.
   - Analyze initial feedback to refine future approaches.

### ASSETS NEEDED

- Customer data list (156 previous buyers).
- Product images and descriptions for deck upgrades.
- Email or messaging platform (e.g., Mailchimp, HubSpot) to send personalized DMs.
- Sales tracking system or CRM tool to monitor responses and close sales.

### METRIC
Conversion Rate from Direct Messages to Sales

### SUCCESS CRITERIA
- At least 5% of targeted customers respond positively to the offer.
- A minimum of 3 sales are made within the first week after sending DMs.
- Positive customer feedback indicating interest in future upgrades or new products.

### IF IT WORKS
- Increased revenue from selling upgraded decks directly to existing customers.
- Enhanced customer engagement and loyalty, potentially leading to repeat business and referrals.
- Valuable insights into customer preferences for product offerings.

### IF IT FLOPS
- Low response rate to DMs (less than 3%).
- No sales or minimal interest shown by targeted customers.
- Negative feedback suggesting that the approach is not well-received.
