### EXPERIMENT SNAPSHOT

**Experiment Name:** Test Commander Deck Upgrades Sales to Existing Buyers  
**Problem/Oppportunity:** Increase revenue and customer lifetime value by upselling premium Commander deck upgrades.  
**Target Silo:** E-Commerce / Direct Mail Marketing  
**Hypothesis:** Targeted DMs promoting exclusive, high-quality Commander deck upgrades will result in at least 20% of previous buyers making a purchase within the next month.

### CHECKLIST

1. **Data Segmentation (15 min)**
   - [ ] Segment recent Commander deck purchasers from existing buyer list.
   
2. **Content Creation (20 min)**
   - [ ] Design personalized DMs highlighting exclusive deals, emphasizing value and exclusivity.
   
3. **DM Production (10 min)**
   - [ ] Use design tool to create visually appealing DMs.
   
4. **Targeted Mailout (5 min)**
   - [ ] Send DMs to 156 selected customers via email marketing platform.
   
5. **Tracking Setup (5 min)**
   - [ ] Set up tracking using analytics tools like Google Analytics or CRM software.

### ASSETS TO PREP

- Existing buyer list of 156 customers
- Graphic design tool or templates for creating DMs
- Email marketing platform (e.g., Mailchimp)
- Tracking and analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics)

### METRIC

**Conversion Rate:** The percentage of recipients making a purchase after receiving the DM.

### SUCCESS LINE

- Achieve at least 20% conversion rate.
- Increase monthly revenue by $1,560 (assuming an average upgrade cost of $30).

### IF IT WORKS

- Positive customer feedback and willingness to purchase additional products or services.
- Increased customer lifetime value and retention.

### IF IT FLOPS

- Low open rates or click-through rates on DMs.
- No increase in sales, indicating disinterest in Commander deck upgrades.
- Negative customer response or complaints.

---

**To Execute:**

1. **Data Segmentation (15 min)**
   - Use your CRM software to filter and segment the 156 recent Commander deck purchasers from your existing buyer list.

2. **Content Creation (20 min)**
   - Utilize a design tool like Canva or Adobe Spark to create personalized DMs that highlight exclusive deals, emphasizing value and exclusivity.
   
3. **DM Production (10 min)**
   - Design the DMs with a focus on creating a visually appealing layout that captures attention.

4. **Targeted Mailout (5 min)**
   - Use your email marketing platform to send out the personalized DMs to 156 selected customers via email.

5. **Tracking Setup (5 min)**
   - Set up tracking within Google Analytics or use the built-in analytics tools in your CRM software to monitor which recipients open and click on the DMs, as well as those who make a purchase.

---

**Post-Experiment Actions:**

1. Review the results of the experiment.
2. Analyze conversion rates, sales figures, and customer feedback.
3. Adjust future strategies based on outcomes (e.g., modify content or target different segments).
4. Communicate findings to stakeholders and plan for potential follow-up experiments.

---

**Notes:** Ensure you have a backup design in case initial efforts do not yield the desired results.
